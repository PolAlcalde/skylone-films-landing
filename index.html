Energy, rest, and focus are part of the craft.

I keep rituals before every shoot: early walks, no noise, and a clear shot list. I choose fewer projects, then show up with full attention. Editing is a form of meditation — I cut until the story feels quiet and true. I’m learning that balance builds better work, and that a calm mind sees better light. Un día a la vez.
